Boot process scheme:

Kernel boot  |_> root=PARTLABEL=rootfs
             |_> init=/sbin/preinit
             |   |_> mount PARTLABEL=configfs  ->  mtdblkXp4
             |   |_> mount overlayfs (configfs as backend) for /etc /usr /root
             \_> init (systemd)
                 |_> mount PARTLABEL=overlayfs
                 \_> mount overlayfs (overlayfs as backend) for /home /var
